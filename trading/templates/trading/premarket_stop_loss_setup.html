Pre-Market Stop Loss Guide
Why Set Stop Losses Before Market Open?
  • Protect against overnight gaps
  • Ensure orders are active immediately when market opens
  • Manage risk while you sleep
  • Avoid emotional trading decisions
Order Types:
  • Stop Loss: Sells at market price when stop price is hit
  • Stop Limit: Sells at limit price or better when triggered
  • Trailing Stop: Adjusts stop price as stock moves in your favor
Best Practices:
  • Set stop loss 5-10% below your entry price
  • Consider recent support levels
  • Account for overnight volatility
  • Use trailing stops for trending stocks
